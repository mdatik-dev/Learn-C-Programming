1. Arithmetic Operators
Operator	Meaning
+	Addition
-	Subtraction
*	Multiplication
/	Division
%	Modulus (Remainder)


2. Relational (Comparison) Operators
Operator	Meaning
==	Equal to
!=	Not equal
>	Greater than
<	Less than
>=	Greater than or equal
<=	Less than or equal


3. Logical Operators
Operator	Meaning
&&	AND
`	
!	NOT


4. Assignment Operators
Operator	Example
=	a = 10
+=	a += 5
-=	a -= 5
*=	a *= 5
/=	a /= 5
%=	a %= 5


5. Increment and Decrement Operators
Operator	Meaning
++	Increase by 1
--	Decrease by 1


6. Bitwise Operators
Operator	Meaning
&	AND
`	`
^	XOR
~	NOT
<<	Left Shift
>>	Right Shift


Most Used Operators
Arithmetic → + - * / %
Comparison → == != > < >= <=
Logical → && || !
Assignment → = += -= *= /=
Increment/Decrement → ++ --
Ternary → ?:
Size → sizeof()
Address → &
Pointer → *
Bitwise → & | ^ ~ << >>
